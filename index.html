About the venue

Space, comfort, and care for every occasion

Madhur Milan has long been a trusted name for weddings, receptions, and family functions in Farrukhabad. Our open lawn accommodates large gatherings with ease, while the air-conditioned banquet hall offers a comfortable indoor setting for ceremonies, dinners, and celebrations of any size.

Generous parking and a central location opposite Christian College, Barhpur, make it simple for guests to arrive — and easy for you to focus on the celebration itself.
